INSTRUCTIONS

The game is simple to play: ARROW KEYS are used to MOVE the ship around and the SPACE-BAR is used to shoot the laser.

The object of the game is, destroy the asteroids before they hit you.

With each level that you beat, the amount of asteroids increases and the tempo of the music also increases, which adds to the excitment. Can you be the best and beat your friend's highscores?

PLAY!
